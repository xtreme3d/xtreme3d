Authors of Xtreme3D

Author of the idea, original API and developer of Xtreme3D up to version 2.0.2.0 inclusive - Xception.

The main developer of Xtreme3D 3.x-4.x is Gecko.

The developer of Xtreme3D 3.8, the author of the functions Pipe, TilePlane, VerletWorld, additional functions Viewer, Light, Grid, Material, Actor, Freeform, HUDSprite, Sprite, FireFX, Lines, Movement - FireRun

The author of the functions DCE and Input is Rutraple aka Hacker.

The author of the functions Grid is Ghost.

The author of the function WindowSetIcon is gadmaker.

Special thanks to gadmaker, van88268, FireRun, JunkerKun, Alexander Demchenko and other Xtreme3D community members for bug reports, valuable ideas and constructive suggestions.

We also express our deepest gratitude to Mike Lischke, Eric Grange and all the contributors to the GLScene project for creating such a wonderful free graphics library.

If you want to join the Xtreme3D development team, send your patches to the project repository on GitHub: https://github.com/xtreme3d/xtreme3d.

You can also share your ideas and suggestions on our forum: https://xtreme3d.ru/forum.
